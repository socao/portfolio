The Dead Fish

Simply fish'licious.

Your local tavern for all your fishy cravings! The Dead Fish was established over a decade ago by the infamous pirate captain, Cranky Biscuit. Since then, we have been proudly serving the community with good-quality customer service and delicious unforgettable food.

We pride ourselves in always using fresh, locally sourced ingredients to create the delicious dishes that you love. Everything is made with love, passion, sweat, grit, and sea salt.

Not only is The Dead Fish an exellent spot for chops, it's also the community and hub for Scallies and Sunnies to drop by and share a drink or two. Whether you're a pirate or a traveler, a merchant, or cityfolk, we welcome you with open arms and a hearty fish dish. Make yourself at home at the most highly rated tavern of the Archipelago!
